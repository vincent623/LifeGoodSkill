# Self-Assessment Assistant

Get objective feedback on your self-evaluation with actionable improvement suggestions.

## Usage

```bash
# Share your self-reflection or evaluation
/life-self-assessment

# Paste your self-assessment when prompted
# e.g., "I think I'm very good at communication skills..."
```

## What It Does

1. **Analyzes** your self-evaluation for accuracy
2. **Identifies** potential overestimation or underestimation
3. **Provides** clear feedback with evidence-based insights
4. **Suggests** concrete improvement recommendations
5. **Rates** relevant competencies with context

## Output

- Objective assessment of your self-evaluation
- Confidence level indicators
- Specific improvement suggestions
- Competency ratings with explanations

## Best For

- Personal development reviews
- Performance self-reflection
- Skill gap analysis
- Career planning
- Growth mindset cultivation
